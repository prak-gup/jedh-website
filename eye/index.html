Common Eye Conditions We Treat

Cataract

Clouding of the eye's natural lens causing blurry vision. Most common in people over 60. Treated with quick, painless surgery. We offer advanced phaco and femto procedures with premium lens options.

Refractive Errors

Myopia (nearsightedness), hyperopia (farsightedness), astigmatism. Causes difficulty focusing. Corrected with glasses, contact lenses, or LASIK/PRK surgery. We offer free screening to check eligibility.

Glaucoma

'Silent thief of sight' - damages optic nerve due to high eye pressure. Often no early symptoms. Regular screening crucial. Managed with eye drops, laser, or surgery to prevent vision loss.

Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetes-related damage to retinal blood vessels. Can lead to blindness if untreated. All diabetics need annual eye exams. We provide comprehensive screening, laser treatment, and anti-VEGF injections.

Dry Eye Syndrome

Insufficient tear production or poor tear quality. Causes discomfort, redness, blurry vision. Common with screen use and aging. Managed with artificial tears, punctal plugs, IPL therapy, and lifestyle modifications.

Pediatric Myopia

Progressive nearsightedness in children. Increasing rapidly due to screen time. Can be slowed with atropine drops, special contact lenses, or lifestyle changes. Early intervention crucial to prevent high myopia.
