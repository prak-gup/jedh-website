Common Dental Problems We Treat

Missing Teeth

Lost teeth due to decay, injury, or gum disease. Affects chewing, speech, and confidence. We offer multiple solutions: dental implants (most permanent), bridges, or dentures. Implants look and feel most natural.

Crooked or Misaligned Teeth

Teeth that don't align properly. Can cause bite issues, cleaning difficulties, and aesthetic concerns. Treated with Invisalign (invisible) or traditional braces. Invisalign preferred by adults for discretion.

Stained or Discolored Teeth

Yellow, brown, or gray teeth from coffee, tea, smoking, or aging. Impacts smile confidence. Professional whitening provides dramatic results (3-8 shades lighter). Veneers option for severe staining or chips.

Tooth Decay (Cavities)

Holes in teeth from bacterial acid. Causes pain and sensitivity. Treated with fillings (tooth-colored composite), crowns for larger cavities, or root canal if infection reaches nerve. Prevention through regular checkups.

Gum Disease (Periodontitis)

Infection of gums causing bleeding, swelling, bad breath. Can lead to tooth loss if untreated. Managed with professional cleaning (scaling), deep cleaning (root planing), antibiotics. Regular maintenance crucial.

Sensitive Teeth

Sharp pain when consuming hot, cold, sweet, or acidic foods. Caused by exposed dentin or enamel erosion. Treated with desensitizing toothpaste, fluoride treatment, bonding, or gum grafts if recession present.
