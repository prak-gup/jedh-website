What is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a revolutionary orthodontic treatment that uses a series of clear, removable aligners to straighten teeth without traditional metal braces. Each aligner is custom-made using 3D digital scanning and gradually moves your teeth into the desired position.

Why Choose Invisalign?

  • Nearly invisible - no one will notice
  • Removable for eating & brushing
  • No food restrictions
  • Comfortable - no metal brackets/wires
  • Fewer dental visits required
  • See your results before starting

How to Wear & Care for Aligners

Daily Wear Schedule:

  • Wear 22 hours per day (minimum 20 hours)
  • Remove only for eating & brushing
  • Change to next aligner every 1-2 weeks
  • Wear while sleeping

Care Instructions:

  • Rinse with lukewarm water after removal
  • Clean with soft toothbrush daily
  • Store in provided case when not worn
  • Avoid hot water (can warp aligners)
  • Brush teeth before reinserting

What You Can Do:

  • Eat anything you want
  • Drink water with aligners in
  • Speak normally (minor adjustment)
  • Play sports & exercise

Pro Tips for Success

  • Set phone reminders for aligner changes
  • Keep case with you always
  • Take photos to track progress
  • Use 'chewies' to ensure proper fit
  • Remove carefully with clean hands

Important: Consistency is key! The more you wear your aligners, the faster and better your results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Invisalign cost?

Treatment costs between ₹75,000 and ₹2,50,000 depending on case complexity and treatment duration. Simple cases (6-8 months) cost less, while comprehensive treatment (12-18 months) costs more. We offer EMI options and accept insurance.

How long does Invisalign treatment take?

Treatment duration varies from 6-18 months depending on your case. Simple spacing/crowding: 6-8 months. Moderate cases: 10-14 months. Complex alignment: 15-18 months. We'll give you exact timeline after digital planning.

Does Invisalign hurt?

Invisalign is generally more comfortable than braces. You may feel pressure when starting a new aligner set - this means it's working! The pressure subsides within 1-2 days. No sharp wires or brackets to cause mouth sores.

Can I eat with Invisalign?

Remove aligners before eating anything except water. This means NO food restrictions! Eat whatever you want. Just brush teeth before reinserting aligners. Total eating time per day: about 2 hours for all meals.

Will Invisalign affect my speech?

You may notice a slight lisp for the first 1-2 days as your tongue adjusts. Most people adapt within 24-48 hours and speech returns to completely normal. Practice speaking and reading aloud to adjust faster.

What happens if I lose an aligner?

Contact us immediately. If you're near the end of that aligner's cycle, we may advance you to the next one. Otherwise, we can order a replacement (takes 1-2 weeks, additional cost applies). Always keep your previous aligner as backup.
