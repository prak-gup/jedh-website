### Pricing Ranges Mentioned (indicative)
- Eye
  - Cataract surgery: Monofocal from ₹18,000/eye; Multifocal/Premium ₹40,000–₹125,000/eye
  - LASIK/Contoura Vision: ₹35,000–₹70,000 per eye (candidate dependent)
- Dental
  - Implants: ₹25,000–₹100,000 per tooth (by system/material)
  - Root Canal: ₹4,000–₹8,000 per tooth (complexity/location)
  - Crowns: ₹5,000 (metal-ceramic) to ₹12,000–₹18,000 (Zirconia/Emax/CAD-CAM)
  - Whitening: ₹10,000–₹25,000 (laser/advanced bleaching)
  - Invisalign: typically ₹75,000+ (case dependent)

### Recovery/Experience Highlights
- Cataract: painless; typical routine resumption in 2–3 days; full recovery ~2–3 weeks.
- LASIK: procedure 10–15 minutes; routine in 24–48 hours.
- Dental: emphasis on painless care (digital anesthesia/laser), same-day CAD-CAM crowns.
- Senior-friendly workflows and assisted experience.

### Insurance/Programs Mentioned
- Cashless facilities: RGHS, CGHS, and major health insurers (as noted on Ophthalmology page).
- Combined Eye + Dental packages for NRI/international patients; assistance with hotel tie-ups and remote follow-ups.
