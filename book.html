Booking FAQs

Is there a consultation fee?

Yes, there's a nominal consultation fee of ₹500-₹1000 depending on the specialty. This fee is adjusted if you proceed with treatment.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule by calling us or messaging on WhatsApp at least 24 hours before your appointment.

What should I bring for consultation?

Bring any previous medical reports, prescriptions, insurance card, and a list of current medications you're taking.

Do you see emergency cases?

Yes, we handle emergency cases during working hours (Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 7 PM). Call us immediately for urgent eye or dental emergencies.

Need Help Booking?
