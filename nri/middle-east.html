  • Dubai to Jaipur: Direct flights (3.5 hours)
  • Airlines: Air India, IndiGo, SpiceJet

  • Multiple daily flights available
  • Return fares: 1,200-2,000 AED

From Saudi Arabia

  • Riyadh to Jaipur: Via Delhi (4-5 hours total)
  • Jeddah to Delhi: Direct flights (4.5 hours)
  • Airlines: Saudi, Air India, IndiGo
  • Return fares: 1,500-2,500 SAR

From Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain

  • Doha to Delhi: Direct (3.5 hours)
  • Kuwait/Bahrain: Via Delhi or direct to Jaipur
  • Multiple connections daily
  • Very convenient for medical tourism

Visa & Travel Requirements

E-Tourist Visa

  • Available for UAE, Saudi, Qatar residents
  • Apply online at: indianvisaonline.gov.in
  • Processing: 3-5 business days
  • Valid for 1 year, 90-day stay per visit
  • Cost: ~200-300 AED equivalent

What You Need

  • Passport (6+ months validity)
  • Passport photo (digital)
  • Residence permit (for Gulf residents)
  • Return flight booking
  • We provide invitation letter if needed

GCC Citizens

GCC nationals may get visa on arrival at certain Indian airports. Check latest regulations before travel.

Common Questions from Gulf Patients

How quick can I schedule treatment after arriving?

Very quick! With pre-arrival WhatsApp consultation, we can schedule your procedure for day 1 or 2 after arrival. Most screenings done same day. This makes it perfect for short trips from Gulf.

Do you have Arabic-speaking staff?

Our primary language is English (widely spoken in Gulf). We can arrange Arabic translation for key consultations if needed. Many patients from Gulf find English communication completely comfortable.

Can I pray at the hospital?

Yes, we have a prayer room available. We respect religious requirements and can direct you to nearby mosques. Hotel staff can also assist with prayer times and Qibla direction.

Is Halal food easily available?

Yes! Jaipur has many Halal restaurants. Hotel restaurants can prepare Halal meals. We can provide list of reliable Halal eateries near your hotel. Vegetarian options also abundant in India.

What if I need to extend my stay?

Not a problem. We can help extend hotel booking. Your e-Tourist visa allows 90-day stay. Most procedures don't need extension, but we're flexible if recovery needs more time or you want to sightsee.

How do payments work for Gulf residents?

We accept AED, SAR, USD, EUR, and INR. International wire transfer, credit cards, or cash all accepted. We provide quote in your currency. Many Gulf patients find it very affordable compared to home country costs.
