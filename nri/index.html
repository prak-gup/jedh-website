How It Works: Pre-Arrival to Recovery

Simple 5-step process for international patients

1. WhatsApp Consultation

Message us on WhatsApp with your medical history, current condition, and photos/reports. We'll provide initial assessment and cost estimate within 24 hours.

2. Video Consultation

Schedule a detailed video call with our doctors. Discuss treatment options, timeline, and get answers to all your questions. Receive confirmed treatment plan.

3. Travel Planning

We help with hotel booking near hospital, airport pickup arrangements, and provide detailed visit itinerary. Share city guide and tips for Jaipur.

4. Treatment in Jaipur

Arrive and start treatment as scheduled. Our team provides daily support, coordinates appointments, and ensures comfort throughout your stay.

5. Post-Treatment Care

Continue recovery at home with remote follow-ups via WhatsApp/video. Receive detailed reports, prescriptions, and 24/7 support for any concerns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from international patients

Do I need a medical visa for treatment?

Most countries can enter India on e-Tourist visa which allows medical treatment. Medical visa is available for longer stays. We provide invitation letter if needed for visa application.

What if I have complications after returning home?

We provide 24/7 WhatsApp support and can arrange video consultations. For serious issues, we can coordinate with local doctors in your area. Most procedures have minimal complication risks.

Can I bring a companion/family member?

Yes, companions are welcome and encouraged. We can arrange accommodation and airport pickup for companions. Family members can be present during consultations.

How do I submit insurance claims?

We provide detailed itemized invoices, medical reports, and all necessary documentation. While we don't directly bill international insurance, our documents support your claims process.

What languages do you speak?

Our doctors and staff speak fluent English and Hindi. We can arrange Arabic translation for Middle East patients if needed. All documentation provided in English.

What about COVID-19 safety protocols?

We follow strict sanitization protocols, maintain social distancing, and ensure staff vaccination. Masks required in clinical areas. We can provide COVID testing if needed.
