Travel Information for US Patients

Visa Requirements

  • US citizens can get e-Tourist visa online
  • Valid for 1 year with 90-day stay per visit
  • Apply at: indianvisaonline.gov.in
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days
  • Cost: $80 (as of 2025)

Flights to Jaipur

  • Fly to Delhi (DEL) - 15+ direct flights from USA
  • Delhi to Jaipur: 3-hour drive or 45-min flight
  • Or fly to Mumbai/Bangalore and connect to Jaipur
  • Typical flight cost: $800-1,500 round-trip
  • Best time to visit: Oct-March (pleasant weather)

Time Zone Considerations

  • India is 10.5 hours ahead of EST
  • WhatsApp consultations available flexible hours
  • Video calls scheduled per your convenience

What to Expect: Jaipur Logistics for Americans

Practical information to make your stay comfortable

Accommodation

Hotels near hospital: $20-120/night. Options from budget to 4-star. All with AC, WiFi, English-speaking staff. We provide recommendations and can assist booking.

Food & Dining

American chains available (McDonald's, Subway, KFC). Indian cuisine recommended (vegetarian-friendly). Hotel restaurants serve continental food. Bottled water widely available.

Connectivity

Free WiFi at hotels and hospital. Local SIM cards available at airport (~$10 for month). WhatsApp works perfectly. ATMs accept US cards (inform bank before travel).

Transportation

We arrange airport pickup. Uber/Ola (like Lyft) available. Private car rental with driver: $30-50/day. Hospital-hotel distance: 2-5 km depending on your hotel.

Language

English widely spoken in Jaipur. All our doctors and staff fluent in English. Hospital signage in English. Most hotels and restaurants have English menus.

Tourism

Visit Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal. Shop for textiles, jewelry. Experience Rajasthani cuisine. Safe, tourist-friendly city. We can arrange guided tours.

Common Questions from US Patients

Is the quality really comparable to USA?

Yes. Our doctors are MS/MDS qualified with international fellowships. Equipment is FDA-approved brands (same as US hospitals). Materials like dental implants are German systems. Lower cost is due to lower overhead and India's economy, not lower quality.

What if I have complications after returning to USA?

We provide 24/7 WhatsApp support and video consultations. For serious issues, we coordinate with US doctors (rare with our procedures). We give detailed medical records. Complication rates are same as US standards.

Can I use my HSA or FSA funds?

Yes! HSA and FSA can be used for qualified medical expenses abroad. We provide itemized receipts and medical documentation that satisfies IRS requirements for reimbursement.

How do I coordinate time off work?

Most procedures need 7-14 days total (including travel). We schedule efficiently to minimize time. Many patients combine with India visit or vacation. We provide employer letters if needed.

What about COVID and health safety?

We follow strict sanitization protocols. Staff fully vaccinated. Masks required in clinical areas. Jaipur has modern hospitals and India's healthcare infrastructure handled COVID well. We can arrange testing if required.

Can my spouse/family come with me?

Absolutely! We encourage companions. They can stay at same hotel, accompany you to appointments, and enjoy Jaipur sightseeing while you recover. Family packages available for multiple patients.
